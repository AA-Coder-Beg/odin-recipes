Easy Peanut Butter Fudge

Total Time: 15 min Prep- 15 min

This peanut butter fudge recipe lives up to its easy title!

An unbelievably easy recipe with peanut butter & marshmallow creme


Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons butter, softened
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 jar (7 oz) marshmallow creme
  • 1-1/3 cups peanut butter

Directions

  1. Line a 8-in square pan with foil and grease with butter.
  2. In a heavy saucepan, combine sugar and milk; bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. Boil for 3 minutes, then remove from heat.
  3. Stir in peanut butter and marshmallow creme until blended. Immediately spread into prepared pan; cool slightly
  4. Refigerate until firm; Using foil, lift fudge out of pan. Remove foil; cut into squares. Store between layers of waxed paper in an airtight container.

Nutrition Factss

1 piece: 99 calories, 4g fat (2g saturated), 7mg cholesterol, 39mg sodium, 15g carbohydrate (14g sugar), 1g protein
